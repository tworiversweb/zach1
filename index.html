I HELP MY CLIENTS PURSUE LIFE, LIBERTY, AND HAPPINESS.

The Law Office of Zachary E. Pedrazzi, LLC, is a boutique practice in downtown Portland that focuses on immigration law, and criminal defense. I take great pride in helping my clients achieve and defend their most important life goals.

Strong client relationships are the foundation of my practice. Excellent client communication enables me to understand my clients' circumstances and goals and to provide high quality, effective representation.

The Law Office of Zachary E. Pedrazzi, LLC, represents clients in Oregon and Washington.

IMMIGRATION

I work to connect people, places and families by helping my clients navigate the complex and shifting landscape of immigration law. Some of the services I provide include adjustment of status; citizenship and naturalization; visas for crime victims and victims of domestic violence; student and tourist visas; asylum; appeals; and removal defense.
